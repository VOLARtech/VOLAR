01 — Mission

Surveillance and patrol that works now

The MK-1 is a working ISR (Intelligence, Surveillance, Reconnaissance) asset that delivers persistent maritime patrol capability under remote operator control over LTE/5G. It doesn't wait for autonomy to be useful. It patrols harbours, monitors waterways, inspects infrastructure, and surveys the environment — today.

While conducting real patrol missions, the MK-1 continuously records every sensor feed, every operator decision, and every environmental condition. This operational dataset — generated as a natural byproduct of real missions — is the training corpus for a separate autonomous AI program. The MK-1 builds the dataset that makes the autonomous USV possible.

02 — Platform

Built for the real world

The MK-1 is a 13-foot hybrid electric USV with a DC generator range extender. Dual azimuth stern drive pods provide 360-degree maneuverability. A 4-sensor EO/IR gimbal delivers surveillance-grade imagery. And the entire vessel is remotely operated over LTE/5G from any location with cellular coverage.
